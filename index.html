France. A small village in the hills of the Périgord Noir, in the Dordogne. A house in the valley. A little corner of life and love and culture called the Manoir de la Moissie, where some days last forever. And they move through the seasons, the weather, the day and the night. It all begins with the resonance of the rooms of the house and its garden. And countless field recordings - people, empty rooms, animals, sunny and rainy days - that keep a moment in time alive. And they go on, perpetually, the way life does.
